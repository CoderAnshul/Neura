Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about NeuraHost services and solutions. Can't find what you're looking for? Contact our support team.

What types of hosting does NeuraHost offer?
NeuraHost offers a variety of hosting solutions to meet your needs, including:

• Shared Hosting - Perfect for smaller websites and beginners
• VPS Hosting - For growing businesses requiring more resources
• Dedicated Servers - Maximum performance for high-traffic sites
• Cloud Hosting - Scalable solutions with guaranteed uptime
• WordPress Optimized Hosting - Specifically tuned for WordPress sites
How long does it take to set up my hosting account?
Your hosting account is typically activated within minutes after we receive your payment. For VPS and dedicated servers, the setup process may take up to 24 hours as we configure your custom environment. You'll receive login details via email as soon as your account is ready to use.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept a wide range of payment methods including all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, bank transfers, and cryptocurrencies including Bitcoin, Ethereum, and Litecoin. All payments are processed securely through our PCI-compliant payment gateway.
Do you offer refunds if I'm not satisfied?
Yes, NeuraHost offers a 30-day money-back guarantee for hosting services. If you're not completely satisfied with our services within the first 30 days, you can request a full refund of your hosting fees. Domain registrations, SSL certificates, and add-on services are non-refundable due to their immediate provision.
How does NeuraHost ensure my website's security?
NeuraHost implements multiple layers of security to protect your website:

• Enterprise-grade firewalls to block malicious traffic
• DDoS protection for all hosting plans
• Free SSL certificates for secure connections
• Automated malware scanning and removal
• Regular security patches and updates
• Daily and weekly backups stored in separate locations
• Two-factor authentication for account access
What uptime does NeuraHost guarantee?
NeuraHost guarantees 99.9% uptime for all our hosting services. Our infrastructure is built on redundant systems with automatic failover capabilities. In the rare event that we don't meet our uptime guarantee, you'll receive credits based on our Service Level Agreement (SLA). We maintain transparency by publishing our real-time server status on our website.
How fast are NeuraHost servers?
NeuraHost servers utilize the latest NVMe SSD storage technology, providing up to 10x faster access times compared to traditional SSDs. Our global CDN network ensures content is delivered from the closest server to your visitors, reducing latency. All servers run on optimized configurations with built-in caching, compression, and the latest PHP versions to maximize performance.
Can I upgrade my hosting plan as my website grows?
Absolutely! NeuraHost makes scaling your hosting resources seamless. You can upgrade your plan at any time through your client dashboard with just a few clicks. The transition is handled by our system with zero or minimal downtime, and you'll only pay the prorated difference between your current and new plan. Our support team can also assist with migration if you're upgrading to a different hosting type.
